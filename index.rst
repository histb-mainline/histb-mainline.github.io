What is histb
-------------

**histb** represents the family of `IPTV STB <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Set-top_box>`_ ARM SoCs from `HiSilicon Technology <https://www.hisilicon.com/>`_, a Chinese fabless semiconductor company. For a comprehensive list of products, see `their official introduction <https://www.hisilicon.com/products/smart-media/STB/>`_.

**This project is not related with HiSilicon Technology, nor do its members receive financial support of any form from HiSilicon representatives in any way.**

Why histb?
----------

The SoC series features MCU with IR module, which can hibernate the ARM core and resume when receives remote controls' signal, a feature usually expected on every STB. Proper MCU configuration, alongside CRG module, is crucial to mainline kernel support.
